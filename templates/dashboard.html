Signal Strategy

The Signal strategy analyzes Bonereaper's trading patterns within each 5-minute window. Instead of copying every trade, it aggregates his activity to detect a dominant direction, then applies Kelly criterion sizing based on the entry price tier.

How it works:
1. Scout (30s / 8 trades) — detect the dominant direction early
2. Flip Check (90s / 15+ trades) — if direction changed since scout, skip (unreliable)
3. Kelly Bet — size the bet based on entry price (cheaper = higher payout, riskier)

Follow Strategy

The Follow strategy copies every individual trade from Bonereaper in real-time via the Polymarket RTDS WebSocket feed. Each copied trade is sized using the same Kelly criterion and capital allocation as the Signal strategy, ensuring consistent risk management across both approaches.

Key difference from Signal: Follow copies all trades (higher volume, lower win rate), while Signal only bets on filtered, high-confidence windows.
